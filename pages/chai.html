My Killer Chai

*note:

Ingredients

  • 2 bags black tea (I prefer Yorkshire Gold)
  • 1 stick cinnamon
  • 8-12 green cardamom pods
  • 1 star anise pod
  • 1 tsp whole black peppercorns
  • 1 tsp whole cloves
  • 1 tsp diced candied ginger
  • brown sugar to taste
